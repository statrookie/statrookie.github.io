What do we do?

StatRookie is a competitive fan engagement platform that focuses on education. We use sports analytics as a medium to teach math and data literacy skills. Our content is designed for any fan to gain the confidence to quantify their observations in and out of the sports arena.

How to we accomplish this?

Our approach focuses on making our content authentic, actionable, and engaging.
