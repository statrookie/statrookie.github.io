Use a passion for sports to power an eagerness for learning.


What do we do?

StatRookie is a competitive fan engagement platform that focuses on education. We use sports analytics as a medium to teach math and data literacy skills. Our content is designed for any fan to gain the confidence to quantify their observations in and out of the sports arena.

Why StatRookie?

At StatRookie, we focus on creating great content that fans can use. Our approach specifically focuses on three values that we have as sports fans. First, we use real player data and real-life situations to make our content authentic. Second, we make our content actionable so fans can take what they learn from StatRookie and apply it to their sports interests. And third, we work to present StatRookie content in an engaging way so that fans continue to challenge themselves to keep learning.

Status

We are currently developing StatRookie for basketball, soccer, and baseball and looking for launch partners. Please contact us if you are a team, league, or other representative and we will set up a time to demo our product.
