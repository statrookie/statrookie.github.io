StatRookie enables any fan to quantify their observations.

We are currently developing StatRookie content for Basketball, Soccer, and Baseball.

What we offer

  • Monthly web and mobile modules that help fans improve their knowledge of specific sports (basketball, soccer, and baseball), math, and data literacy.
  • An experience that uses real player and team statistics to create an authentic and engaging learning experience.
  • Advanced concepts in a simple but engaging form for fans to explore and enjoy.
  • A variety of topics from basic proability to game theory.
